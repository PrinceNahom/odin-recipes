Gomen-besiga

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-large bowl spinach
  • ½ kg beef
  • 3 sliced onions
  • 3 cloves crushed garlic
  • 3 green chili pepper
  • ½ tbsp black pepper
  • 3 tbsp niter kibbeh (Ethiopian clarified butter)

Steps

  1. Begin by preheating a pot, then add the beef to it.
  2. When the beef browns, add in the garlic and onions and continue cooking for around 5 minutes.
  3. Next, add the spinach and continue cooking and stirring for 15 more minutes.
  4. Finally, add the black pepper, and niter kibbeh to the mix, continue cooking for around 3 more minutes and add some salt.
